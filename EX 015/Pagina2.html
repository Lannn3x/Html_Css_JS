John: The joy inside me is like a cup of coffee, always present on the corner of the table. The aroma that was once disheartening now spreads through the air, bringing sweet and comforting memories. Each sip reminds me of the warmth and companionship that fill my body and soul, bringing a feeling of comfort and happiness.
